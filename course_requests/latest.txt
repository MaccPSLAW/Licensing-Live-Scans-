  ABOUT MACCESS INC.
  MACCESS INC. is a California-based provider of professional
security guard training and licensing support. We deliver
BSIS-compliant instruction designed to prepare security
professionals for safe, lawful, and confident performance in
the field. Our courses combine clear legal grounding with
practical, real-world application.

  COURSE OVERVIEW
  The Power to Arrest 8-Hour Training is a mandatory course
required by the California BSIS for all individuals seeking a
Security Guard registration (guard card). This course provides
the legal and practical foundation a security officer needs to
understand their authority, responsibilities, and limitations
while on duty.

WHO THIS COURSE IS FOR
- New applicants pursuing a California BSIS guard card
- Security professionals refreshing their Power to Arrest
  knowledge
- Employers seeking compliant, well-trained personnel

LEARNING OBJECTIVES
Upon completion, participants will be able to:
1. Define the legal authority and limits of a security guard's
   power to arrest.
2. Distinguish between the role of a security officer and that
   of a peace officer.
3. Identify when a citizen's arrest is lawful and how to carry
   it out properly.
4. Apply appropriate use-of-force standards within legal
   boundaries.
5. Respond to incidents while protecting the rights and safety
   of all parties.
6. Document and report incidents accurately and professionally.

COURSE OUTLINE (8 HOURS)
1. Responsibilities and Ethics in Citizen Arrest
2. Relationship Between a Security Guard and a Peace Officer
3. Limitations on Security Guard Power to Arrest
4. Restrictions on Searches and Seizures
5. Trespass Law
6. Ethics and Professional Conduct
7. Use of Force and Legal Standards
8. Incident Documentation, Reporting, and Examination

CERTIFICATION & COMPLIANCE
This training meets the California BSIS requirements for the
Power to Arrest course. Upon successful completion, students
receive a certificate of completion that satisfies the
applicable BSIS training requirement for guard card
registration.
